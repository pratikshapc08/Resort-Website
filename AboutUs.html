About Our Restaurant

Welcome to Blue Bella Resort!

Nestled in the heart of Pune, Blue Bella Resort is the perfect getaway for those seeking a peaceful retreat surrounded by natural beauty. Whether you’re looking for a relaxing vacation, a family adventure, or a romantic escape, our resort offers the ideal blend of luxury and comfort.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Blue Bella Resort was created with a vision of providing world-class hospitality while staying close to nature. Over the years, we’ve grown from a small family-run establishment into one of the top-rated resorts in the region, known for our exceptional service and unforgettable experiences.

Our Service

At Blue Bella Resort, we offer:

  • Luxurious Rooms: Enjoy the comfort of our well-appointed rooms, designed to cater to every need with breathtaking views of the surroundings.
  • Gourmet Dining: Our on-site restaurant serves a variety of delectable cuisines, featuring fresh, locally sourced ingredients.
  • Activities & Experiences: From guided nature walks to adventure sports and spa services, we have something for everyone.
  • Event Hosting: Whether it's a wedding, corporate retreat, or special occasion, our team will ensure your event is unforgettable.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to create lasting memories for each guest through personalized experiences, excellent service, and a commitment to sustainability. We believe in protecting the natural environment and promoting eco-friendly practices that align with our guests' desire for responsible tourism.
